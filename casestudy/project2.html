Project 2: A Rainy Day

Expressive
Poem

Click to visit project website

For this project, I created a poem using different effects and changes, in order to make the text more expressive and make readers immersive and engaged while reading. I chose Tagore's poetry, On a rainy day. It described the scenery of a evening that was about to rain and the gently calling to the c hildren who went out to play.

The color of text will be changing when scrolling because I created a background-clip for the text, and gave it a background color in gradient. The effect on title was a change in text-shadow color using css. I also did a lot of hover on the hightlighted text, with different kinds of effects.
